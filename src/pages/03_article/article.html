01

On Interaction

An interaction is often likened to a conversation - the two interacting agents have to:
a) listen to each other,
b)understand what the other agent is saying, and
c) respond to each other in a meaningful way.
Complex interactions take place throughout the natural world, but recently, the advancements in digital technologies enabled machines to interact with humans, as well as with other machines, in ever more complicated ways.
The Interactive Media program at NYU Abu Dhabi leverages the potential of technology to explore and examine the ways we create, communicate, and participate in the world.
